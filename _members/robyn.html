Meet Robyn! This is her second year singing with Halcyon. She is a Regina-based theatre artist, having graduated from both the Globe Theatre Conservatory and the University of Regina. When not pursuing theatre, she works with the Regina Public Library.

Her favourite piece (thus far) is Karl Jenkins's Stabat Mater, which we sang at our last concert, and her favourite memory was from last season where we sang A Night of Brahms with the Regina Symphony Orchestra on stage at the Conexus Arts Centre!
